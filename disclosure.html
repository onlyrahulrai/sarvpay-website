Partner Disclosure

Transparency is the core of our community. Here is how SarvPay sustains and rewards you.

How We Earn & Share

SarvPay (Sarvpay Universe OPC Pvt Ltd) is a participant in various partner marketplace programs. When you click on a brand link (like Amazon, Flipkart, or Myntra) through our app and make a qualifying purchase, we receive a commission from our partner networks such as Impact, Admitad, and Vcommission.

Zero Extra Cost to You

The commission we receive is paid by the merchant. It does not increase the cost of the product or service for you. You pay the same price as you would directly on the merchant's site.

The Reward Model

Unlike traditional platforms, we intend to share a significant portion of this received commission back with our community via our upcoming SarvPay Coins program. This allows you to earn while you shop.
