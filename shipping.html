Detailed Fulfillment Policy

SarvPay does not handle physical inventory, logistics, or courier services. We act solely as a facilitator and aggregator.

Shipment tracking and delivery status for physical orders must be checked directly on the merchant's platform where the purchase was made.

Digital receipts for recharges are available within the "History" section of the SarvPay app immediately after a successful transaction.
